    <refpurpose>Cross reference anchor on top of the <sgmltag>figure</sgmltag> float</refpurpose>
  </refnamediv>
  <refsynopsisdiv>
    <programlisting>
      <xi:include xmlns:xi="http://www.w3.org/2001/XInclude" parse="text" href="syn/figure.anchor.top.xml"/>
    </programlisting>
  </refsynopsisdiv>
  <refsection>
    <title>Description</title>
    <para>By default the anchor of a formal figure is where the figure title
    appears. It means that a link to the figure points after the
    image when the title is below the image, and at the top of the image when
    the title is on the top.
    This parameter allows to put the anchor at the top of the image even if the
    title is below the figure.</para>
    <para>Note that it is not possible to have the anchor below the image
    if the title is on the top.</para>
  </refsection>
